# TasteBite-Sales-Performance-Analysis

TasteBite is an emerging e-commerce platform specializing in fresh, healthy raw foods and natural drinks, offering premium products like flour, candy, canned sweets, cassava starch, coffee, dry wine, and more. This project focuses on building a comprehensive Power BI sales performance dashboard for TasteBite a fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG) brand. The goal was to transform raw transactional data into actionable business intelligence to support executive decision-making, identify revenue drivers, evaluate operational efficiency, and uncover future investment opportunities.

The analysis reveals strong business momentum with total revenue of **$18M**, representing **109% growth**, total quantity sold of **6M units (+128%)**, average pricing strength increasing by **112%**, and a total of **53K customer orders (+131%)**. These indicators show aggressive market traction, strong consumer demand, and effective commercial execution, while also highlighting critical opportunities to scale sustainably.

This dashboard does not just visualize data; it tells a business story, revealing where the company wins, where risks exist, and where leadership must focus next.

---

## Business Problem & Objectives

TasteBite leadership required clarity on key business questions:

* How is the business performing financially and operationally?
* Which products, markets, and channels are driving revenue?
* Which salespeople and supervisors contribute most to business growth?
* How can leadership justify continued investment, expansion, and resource allocation?
* What strategies will sustain growth for the next 2–10 years?

**Primary Objective:** Create a data-driven, executive level dashboard to support strategic decisions, investment confidence, and long-term business growth.

---

## Data Approach & Analytical Process

A structured data workflow was adopted to ensure reliability and business credibility:

### 1. Data Extraction & Cleaning

Sales transaction records were imported into Power BI, ensuring accuracy, consistency, and removal of duplicated or incomplete records.

### 2. Data Modeling & KPI Design

Key metrics were defined to reflect business performance:

* Revenue
* Order Volume
* Quantity Sold
* Pricing Trends
* Customer Reach
* Salesperson & Supervisor Performance
* Channel Contribution
* Yearly Trend Movement

### 3. Business Storytelling & Visualization

Dashboards were designed for executive clarity, visual storytelling, and quick decision-making using:

* KPI Cards for executive performance overview
* Trend lines to understand growth patterns
* Leaderboards to measure team performance
* Distribution analysis for channels and customer segments

Every visual answers a business question, making data meaningful and strategic.
